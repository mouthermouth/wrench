Artemis Rockets

Artemis is a mission which has an aim to establish permanent human settlement on moon and to study it. Mission was acknowledged with the thought to send humans with life supporting techs. The main component of Artemis is SLS rocket series which are a beast. They have the power to lift more than 10000 elephants. Artemis is a great project which would be helping the humans to advance in their skills and make them multiplantery.



Starship Rockets

Starship Rocket is a heavy launch vechile which is under development by SpaceX. SpaceX is a private company which aims at providing cost effective launch vechiles. Starship is a monsterous launch vechile which would take humans to Mars with the motive to help them to achieve the multiplantery stage. Starship is a two stage vechile which can soft land on terrains and even water.



Ariane Series

Ariane Series is a developing and emerging rocket series which consists of five launch vechiles which are capable of transfering GSAT satellites in GSAT orbits. This series is being made possible by space agency of Europe, ESA. Ariane Series aims at providing cost effective and quality assurance launches into space.















GSLV Series

GSLV series is an indigenous series of rockets which is developed by ISRO. GSLV stands for Geosynchronus Satellite Launch Vechiles. GSLV series has aimed at providing launch vechiles which can transfer indigenous developed satellites and other instruments to study behavior of space. GSLV Mk-III is currently India's most powerful launch vechile which is one the cost of Moon mission by NASA known as Apollo mission.
